What is SmartRoll?

SmartRoll is a cutting-edge digital platform designed to modernize the educational experience. By leveraging dynamic QR technology, we've eliminated the need for manual roll calls, allowing teachers to focus on what truly matters: teaching.

Our system provides a unified hub for attendance tracking, resource sharing, and assignment management, ensuring a seamless workflow for both educators and students.

How It Works

1

Generate QR

Teachers generate a unique, time-sensitive QR code for their class session.

2

Scan QR

Students scan the code using their mobile devices to verify presence.

3

Instant Record

Attendance is logged instantly in the secure cloud database.

4

Share & Manage

Notes and assignments are distributed to the class automatically.
